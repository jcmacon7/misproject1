My Hobbies & Interests

Outdoors & Water Activities

I enjoy numerous outdoor activities such as hiking, paddleboarding, kayaking, biking, walking, rollerblading, and rock climbing. Both my hometown, Winona, Minnesota, and Duluth have many opportunities to explore natural beauties through these outdoor activities. Winona and Duluth are both bordered by beautiful bluffs and powerful water sources, with the Mississippi river in Winona and Lake Superior in Duluth. The bluffs provide many stunning hiking trails that always end in a gorgeous view of the surrounding area, and the lake and river provide refreshing places to swim, paddleboard, kayak, and go boating.

Riding Down the Dunes

I have had a lot of fun exploring different parks around the world. Visiting these gorgeous areas have created some of my favorite memories. For example, I went to Great Sand Dunes National Park one summer, and my family got up early one morning, hiked out to the sand dunes, and got to ride down the sand dunes on sand boards and sleds. It was similar to snowboarding, and we had a lot of fun riding down all of the big dunes. We had to go early in the morning before the sun came out and heated the sand up too much. My family was scraped up and tired afterwards, but it was one of most fun and unique activities I have ever done, and I am so grateful I had the opportunity to do it.

So What?

These activities are important to me because they allow me to explore different areas in the US and around the world that I otherwise would have no reason to visit. Some of my most memorable experiences occurred when I got to do different outdoor activities. I also think it is important to protect the natural beauty within our world, and organizations such as the National Parks Service need support from people to continue operating.
