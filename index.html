Hiking

I enjoy numerous outdoor activities, including hiking. I like to find different trails in Minnesota and Wisconsin to hike. Some of my favorite hiking trails include the Holzinger trails in Winona, MN, Grandad's Bluff in La Crosse, WI, Congdon Park in Duluth, MN, and the trails at Devil's Lake State Park in WI.

Rock Climbing

Rock climbing is one of my favorite activities. I have only climbed outside a few times, but I have enjoyed the indoor rock-climbing walls at both the University of Minnesota Duluth and Winona State University in my hometown. Rock climbing is a great sport because climbers can challenge themselves at their own comfort level, gain great upper body strength, and do it year-around because there are indoor and outdoor climbing areas.
