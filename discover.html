University of Minnesota Duluth

Explore what makes UMD a unique and exceptional place for education.

Why UMD

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because it is located in a beautiful area, is affordable, and has many strong programs in the Labovitz School of Business and Economics for students to explore various majors. I also like that UMD is a big enough school that provides impressive opportunities for students, but small enough that students do not just feel like another number. I also like that UMD promotes a positive culture for students to express and explore their interests, which creates a safe and comfortable environment for everyone.

My UMD Experiences

I have not been at UMD very long, but I have really enjoyed my time here so far. I often utilize many beneficial aspects of UMD, including free access to the gym, free bus rides to anywhere in Duluth, the indoor access to almost every building on campus, and the numerous engaging clubs offered. I am involved in the Coffee Club, Book Club, Women in Business Club, and a Bible Study. I also enjoy attending events such as football games, hockey games, and shows at the Marshall W. Alworth planetarium. There are a wide variety of activities and extracurriculars at UMD for all students.
